George Brown College

George Brown College opened in 1968 after being chartered in 1966 by the Ontario government. In its initial years,
George Brown began with two campuses: one near Casa Loma and another on Nassa Street in Kensington Market. Now, It operates a third location, one in 
Waterfront. 

George Brown College, emphasizes career-focused education, and developing programs with industry partners to meet current labor market demands. It 
collaborates with numerous companies and organizations to provide edcuation on the field , advise on the curriculum, and to streamline employment for
graduates
