The COMP_3104 DevOps course provides the students the procedures protocols, and best practices that a DevOps engineer brings to a workplace environment 
to increase efficiency and improve the overall workflow. In this course students will learn to analyze and evaluate various developer operation 
implementations available and implement hands-on work using industry standard worktools.

A student's success in this course will rely on three things: their lab and lecture attendance, the completion of lecture quizzes, and following weekly
lab exercises.

At the end of this course, students will be able to demonstrate knowledge of effective DevOps techniques and be able to providce solutions using industry
standard tools and techniques. Such as CI/CD strategies, software development lifecycle, and application containerization for deployment.
