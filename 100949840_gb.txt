George Brown College (GBC)
--------------------------------
Location: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
George Brown has three campus locations: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront

--------------------------------


What GBC is known for
- Known for applied learning with labs and projects.
- They have strong industry partnerships and have a lot of co-op/internship options depending on your course.
- Some of the programs that are offered are Technology, Business, and Health Sciences.


Why I chose this college:

- I can attend lectures, but I can also review the recordings.
- I can work and study at the same time.
